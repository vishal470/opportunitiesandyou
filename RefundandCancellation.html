Cancellation Policy

At Opportunities And You, we strive to provide excellent services in the field of influencer marketing, with a strong focus on Web Development and search engine optimization (SEO). We understand that circumstances may change, and we want to ensure transparency and clarity regarding our refund and cancellation policy. Please read the following policy carefully:

Read Below
  • 1. Service Agreement:
  • a. When engaging our services, clients will enter into a formal agreement specifying the scope of work, deliverables, timelines, and associated costs.
  • b. The agreement will be the primary reference point for any refund or cancellation requests
  • 2. Cancellation of Services
  • a. Clients may request to cancel our services by providing a written notice to our designated contact person.
  • b. Depending on the stage of the project, cancellation fees may apply, which will be determined on a case-by-case basis.
  • c. Any costs incurred by Opportunities And You up until the cancellation will be invoiced to the client.
  • 3. Refunds:
  • a. Refund requests will be considered under the following circumstances
    • i. If Opportunities And You fails to fulfill the agreed-upon services or deliverables as outlined in the service agreement.
    • ii. If the client provides a written notice of dissatisfaction, clearly stating the reasons for the refund request, and gives Opportunities And You a reasonable opportunity to rectify the issue.
  • b. Refunds will be issued at the sole discretion of Opportunities And You and will be assessed on a case-by-case basis.
  • c. Refunds, if approved, will be processed within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 10 days.
  • 4. Non-Refundable Fees:
  • a. Certain fees associated with third-party services, advertising platforms, or influencer collaborations may be non-refundable. These fees will be clearly communicated to the client before engagement.
  • b. Any costs incurred by Opportunities And You that are non-refundable will be clearly stated in the service agreement.
  • 5. Changes to the Agreement:
  • a. Any changes or modifications to the service agreement must be agreed upon in writing by both parties.
  • b. Opportunities And You reserves the right to update or modify this refund and cancellation policy at its discretion. In such cases, the updated policy will be effective upon posting on our website or notifying the client directly.


We encourage clients to thoroughly review the service agreement and discuss any concerns or queries before engaging our services. Our goal is to provide exceptional influencer marketing solutions, Web Development and assist with your SEO needs.
